Turquastic Automation Project

This application is a personal automation project developed to streamline content management across various social media platforms. Its primary purpose is to facilitate the seamless transfer and publication of visual content from a designated, user-owned Pinterest board to platforms such as Instagram, Facebook, and X.

All automation is performed in strict compliance with the respective platforms' API policies and terms of service. This project does not engage in any form of unauthorized data collection or content scraping.
